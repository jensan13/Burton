THE NIGHTMARE BEFORE CHRISTMAS

What is TNBC?

As you probably already figured out, TNBC stands for The Nightmare Before Christmas.This is one of Tim Burton's movies.

In this movie, Jack Skellington tries to steal Christmas when he encounters the town of Christmas and when he's also tired of doing the same thing for Halloween. He also finds out that Sally was protecting him by helping him not get in trouble and at the end, they both sing their song in the Spiral Hill and stay together!!
